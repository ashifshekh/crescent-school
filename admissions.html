Admissions

A clear path, from first visit to first day

We've kept our admissions process to five simple steps — with a real person available at every one of them.

The process

How admission to Crescent works

1

Explore

Browse our curriculum, facilities and fee structure online, or download the school brochure to share with family.

2

Visit

Book a campus tour to meet our team, see real classrooms in session, and get your questions answered in person.

3

Apply

Submit your enquiry and required documents through our online admissions form below — takes about 5 minutes.

4

Assess

Depending on grade level, your child may be invited for an age-appropriate entrance assessment or a short interaction.

5

Enrol

Once offered a place, complete registration, uniform and transport arrangements ahead of the new term.
