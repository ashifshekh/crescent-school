About us

Four decades of Crescent

Since 1984, Crescent English High School has grown from a single Al Qusais campus into one of Dubai's established CBSE schools — without losing the sense that every student should be known by name.

Our foundations

Vision, mission & values

Vision

To be a leading CBSE institution in Dubai recognised for academic excellence, strong character, and a genuinely caring school community.

Mission

To nurture confident, capable learners who think critically, act with integrity, and contribute meaningfully to their communities.

Values

Excellence, integrity, care, curiosity and community — the five ideas that shape every decision we make.

Chairman's message

"For over four decades, Crescent English High School has stood for one simple idea: that a good school should feel like a second home. Our aim has never just been strong exam results — though our students consistently achieve them — but the shaping of confident, grounded young people who are ready for whatever comes next."
Chairman
Crescent English High School

Principal's message
